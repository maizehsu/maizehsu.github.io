About Me

分享喜欢的诗中的一段话

If you can dream—and not make dreams your master;
If you can think—and not make thoughts your aim;
If you can meet with Triumph and Disaster
And treat those two impostors just the same;
If you can bear to hear the truth you’ve spoken
Twisted by knaves to make a trap for fools,
Or watch the things you gave your life to, broken,
And stoop and build ’em up with worn-out tools:

If —— by Rudyard Kipling

Hey there, welcome to my personal website! I’m Maize, a junior studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. When I’m not busy coding or debugging, you can usually find me jamming on the guitar 🎸, attending live events🎷, watching movies🎬, reading books📖, hiking🥾, or traveling✈️.

I’m a firm believer in using my voice to help those in need, and I try my best to keep up with world events and issues that affect people’s lives. That being said, I’m always open to new perspectives and quality conversations. So if you have something interesting to share or discuss, feel free to hit me up!

I have a deep appreciation for life and an insatiable curiosity about everything around me. I love trying new things, experiencing different cultures, and exploring the unknown. I’m always on the lookout for the next adventure, whether it’s hiking a remote trail or trying out a new cuisine.

Speaking of experiences, I still vividly recall that one magical night at a jazz club, where the talented fingers of a guitarist brought to life a cascade of intricate and soulful notes that seamlessly blended with each other, leaving me absolutely speechless. It was in that moment that I realized how much I cherish music and the arts, and how they enrich our lives in ways we can’t even imagine.

So, to wrap it up, I’m just a guy who’s passionate about life and all the wonderful things it has to offer. If you share the same interests, let’s connect and make some great memories together!

About My Life

Music

🎶 When it comes to music, I have quite the eclectic taste. I love the soulful sounds of jazz and blues, with favorites like Stevie Ray Vaughan, John Mayer, and Kenny Wayne Shepherd. And when I’m in the mood for something with a little more edge, I turn to classic rock and metal, with bands like Beatles, Metallica, Beyond, and 羅大佑.

🎸 Lastly, I’m a big fan of indie pop, indie rock, and post-rock, with some of my go-to’s being MLA, deca joins, 万青, and 草东没有派对. But hey, I’m always open to discovering new artists and genres, so if you have any recommendations, feel free to hit me up!

👉 Some of my favorite music experiences have been:

  • 🌲 Jamming on the guitar with friends around a bonfire at a secluded campsite in Yosemite National Park.
  • 🎤 Attending a live blues concert at a small club in Chicago, where the music was so raw and emotional that it gave me goosebumps.
  • 🎧 Blasting Metallica’s “Master of Puppets” while driving through the winding roads of Zion National Park, feeling like a total badass.

🎵 Music has the power to transport us to another world, to evoke emotions we never knew we had, and to connect us with people from all walks of life. And that’s why I’ll always have a special place in my heart for it.

Movies and Shows and Lives

🍿🎬🎥🎭

Whether it’s on the big screen or the small screen, I’m always looking for captivating stories that transport me to new worlds and inspire me.

🎭 In terms of musicals, “Les Misérables,” “Phantom of the Opera,” and “Hamilton” have all left a lasting impression on me.

🌟 I also have a soft spot for animated fiction series like “Bojack Horseman,” “Rick and Morty,” and “Gravity Falls,” which all tell compelling stories in their own unique ways.

🚀 And when it comes to movies and TV shows, my taste is all over the place! I enjoy everything from sci-fi epics like “Westworld” to fantasy dramas like “A Song of Ice and Fire” and sitcoms like “The Good Place.”

🎉 Whether it’s a live performance by a favorite musician, a stand-up comedy show, or a Broadway play, I always love experiencing the energy and magic of live performances.

If you have any recommendations that fit the bill, I’m all ears!

Places I’ve Hiked 🥾

  • Yellowstone National Park 🏞️: This iconic park has some of the most breathtaking views I’ve ever seen. Hiking the trails around Old Faithful and the Grand Prismatic Spring was an unforgettable experience.
  • Zion National Park 🏜️: The towering sandstone cliffs and deep canyons of Zion made for some challenging but rewarding hikes. The views from Angel’s Landing and The Narrows were simply stunning.
  • Mount Rainier National Park 🏔️: The snow-capped peak of Mount Rainier was a majestic sight as we hiked through the subalpine meadows and forests. The wildflowers in bloom were a riot of colors.
  • Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore 🏖️: The massive dunes along the shore of Lake Michigan were a unique and fun place to hike. The views of the lake and surrounding forests were picture-perfect.
  • Isle Royale National Park 🌲: This remote island in Lake Superior offered some of the most peaceful and secluded hiking I’ve ever done. The pristine forests and rugged coastline were a nature lover’s dream.

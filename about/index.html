About Me

Hello there! I’m Mai, a software engineer by profession, a guitarist by passion, and a minimalist at heart. My career, hobbies, and life philosophy are bound together by my commitment to simplicity, productivity, and personal knowledge management. Currently, I am practicing Stoicism and working on becoming a technical writer and lifelong learner, on a mission to contribute to open source and knowledge sharing.

My educational and personal experiences have shaped my aspiration to effect positive societal change. I aim to craft practical solutions to address real-world issues, either through the development of reliable software systems or by performing inspiring musical composition, each offering unique yet complementary ways to make the world a better place.

Beyond my profession and passion for music, my interests are wide-ranging. To list but a few:

  • Stories in the form of movies, novels, animation, games, and more. I find joy in exploring character arcs and motives across various forms of storytelling;
  • Abstract theories and methodologies fascinate me. This includes books and blogs that dissect life experiences from diverse perspectives, and my interest extends to history and literature;
  • Embracing cultural diversity and different life experiences through trying new things, making friends from various backgrounds, and occasionally traveling, especially on road trips!
  • Experiences that make me feel alive, such as live music, musicals, jazz bars, concerts, and more;
  • Nature’s company, a source of peace through activities like meditation, running and hiking. I aim to visit every (7/63) national parks :)

These identities, aspirations, and interests shape me, together painting a vibrant picture of the life I am striving to lead.

If you share any of these interests, feel free to reach out to me via email. I would love to hear from you!
